Iron Man - 2008

Director Jon Favreau
Players Robert Downey Jr., Samuel L. Jackson

Tony Stark returns home to discover Samuel L. Jackson as Nick Fury, director of S.H.I.E.L.D., who wants him in on a little thing called the “Avengers Initiative”. Jackson was smuggled onto set in complete secrecy. Naturally, news of his cameo leaked everywhere. We blame Hydra spies.

Sets up Avengers Assemble. Right from the off.
