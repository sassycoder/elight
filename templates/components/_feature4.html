The most tragically relevant film of this year’s Oscars, Selma is an ensemble story of the historic civil-rights marches that forced change in the US. David Oyelowo’s Martin Luther King Jr. is the standout, while the carefully composed direction of Ava DuVernay makes the bubbling anger that drives characters and film all the more powerful. HOH
