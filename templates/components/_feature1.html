1 Take A Cut Of The Profits To play the title role in the 1911 Australian film Sweet Nell Of Old Drury, Aussie stage legend Nellie Stewart agreed a fee of £1,000 plus a share of the profits, the first recorded instance of a film star getting a cut of the points. Sly’s Rocky(s), Jack’s Joker, Tom’s Missions and the rest were decades away.
